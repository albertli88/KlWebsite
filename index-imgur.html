Our Mission Statement

The contracting field is hardly a novel one, with hundreds of competitors in the Bay Area alone. If that's the case, why choose KL Construction Group? What makes us stand out is our dedication to being above and beyond by following four principles: Safety, Quality, Timeliness, and Reasonable Budget.

Safety: No one should risk getting hurt in performing their job. The utmost precaution will be taken in all circumstances. We will immediately halt all construction if it is deemed unsafe to work and resume working upon mitigiation of any existing risk.

Quality: We are committed to meet and exceed the quality of work as agreed with our clients. Every effort will be made to achieve or exceed the expectations of our clients.

Timeliness: A promise is a promise. When we commit to a schedule with a client, we will meet the deadline. If we are unable to complete the project before the deadline, we will compensate our clients.

Reasonable Budget: Last but not least, all our projects come at a reasonable budget. Although sometimes the actual price may come to exceed the estimated budget for the project, we take the utmost care in devising a high quality project for the customer.

Our ultimate goal is the customer's satisfaction. Our business philosophy is a win-win situation for both us and the customer, and never a zero-sum game. I am confident that we can make you our next satisfied customer. Give us a call today.

At Your Service

Commercial Renovation

About one third of our renovation work is in the commercial space, including retail, dining and offices. Our firm coordinates very closely with architectural and engineering firms to provide our clients the most cost effective solution to their needs.

Residential Renovation

One of the most practical ways in adding values to an existing property is to update the kitchen and the bathroom. Renovation of existing home is not only a good investment, but also provides the home owners an opportunity to personalize their own space to fit their lifestyle. Our firm is committed to deliver an end product that will meet or exceed our clients' expectations.

New Additions

As property values continue to skyrocket in the Bay Area, there are two cost effective ways to accomodate a expanding family. The first method is to add an addition to an existing home. The second is to purchase a smaller home at a reduced cost and expand the home with additions. Our firm works closely with certified architects to make sure the construction process complies with the home owner's needs.
